My UX Skills

User Research

User research is vital for good interface design. By utlizing, lean design principles and through extensive research and testing I am able to discover what the market is for that specific product and how to best design based on what the target market would be looking for. I do this through identifying the market, interviews, synthesis, user testing and iterate based on continual feedback.

User Interface Design

Through a number of case studies completed in order learn the design process I have learned what works and doesn't work when designing a product on a small scale. Working with a team on a number of these products helped me gain experience with what it might be like with a company design team. I have learned the design process from the beggining starting with identifying the product and the market for that product to then handing the design off to the developement team.
